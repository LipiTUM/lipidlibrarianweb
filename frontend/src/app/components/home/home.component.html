LipidLibrarian

LipidLibrarian is a cross linked lipid database, gathering information on demand from three major lipidomics databases ALEX123, LIPID MAPS, and SwissLipids, as well as enriching the results with data from the LION/web and LINEX projects.

LipidLibrarian is powered by Goslin and LipidLynxX.
